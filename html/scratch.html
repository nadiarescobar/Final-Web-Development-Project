My Scratch Page: Baking

Introduction

I love to bake! It’s one of my favorite hobbies, and on this page, I’ll be sharing some of my favorite recipes that I’ve tried—along with a few that I hope to try soon.

My Favorite Recipes

  1. Apple cinnomon Cake
    • 4 Cups of peeled and sliced granny smith apples
    • 2 Cups of sugar
    • 2 cups of flour
    • 1 1/2 tsp of baking soda
    • 2 tsp of salt
    • 3 eggs
    • 2 tsp of cinnonmon
    • 3/4 of vegetable or coconut oil
    • 2 tsp of vanilla
    Mix ingredients together.Pour into greased pan. Bake at 350 for 50 mins.
  2. Banana chocolate chip Muffins
    • 4 ripe bananas
    • 3/4 unsalted butter
    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 tsp vanilla
    • 1 3/4 of all purpose flour
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1 cup milk chocolate chips
    Mix together and bake at 325 for 13 mins.
